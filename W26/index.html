Engineering Mathematics I

Course Overview

This course introduces fundamental mathematical concepts necessary for engineering disciplines.

Course Objectives

  • Understand differential and integral calculus
  • Apply mathematical methods to solve engineering problems
  • Develop analytical thinking skills

Syllabus

  1. Matrices and Determinants
  2. Differential Calculus
  3. Integral Calculus
  4. Differential Equations
  5. Vector Calculus

Credits

4 Credits (3 Theory + 1 Tutorial)

Contact Hours: 4 hours per week

References

  • Higher Engineering Mathematics by B.S. Grewal
  • Advanced Engineering Mathematics by Erwin Kreyszig
